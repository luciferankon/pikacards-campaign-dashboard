Quick Win Playbook — Do In Order

Each action is backed by Shopify + GA4 data. Ordered by revenue impact and effort.

Day 1 — Quick Wins (Already Have the Tools)

Add Instagram link to site footer + header

Footer has zero social links. 428 Instagram visitors can't find your pack opening content. Your eBay reviews are on the homepage — your Insta should be too.

Promote Appstle loyalty program on product pages

Loyalty widget is installed but hidden. Only 6.4% repeat rate (13/203 buyers). Add "Earn X points on this purchase" on product pages to drive signups.

Optimise cart recovery email sequence

Emails are active but recovery is low on 137 abandoned checkouts. Test 3-email sequence: 1hr (reminder), 6hr (social proof), 24hr (urgency + $10 off).

Day 2–3 — Revenue Diversification

Feature One Piece prominently on homepage

One Piece = $41K (28.9% of revenue) but not shown on homepage "Newly Listed". EB-03 Heroines did $39.4K from just 23 units. Give it homepage real estate.

Create "Bundle Deals" to push orders toward $500

7 orders stuck at $400–499 (just missed free shipping). Create "2-box bundles" or "Complete Set" bundles that land at $500+ to capture the threshold.

Launch Yu-Gi-Oh! Google Shopping campaign

YGO converts at 27x Pokemon rate — $68/view vs $2.50/view. $21.6K revenue from just 24 units. Huge untapped ad opportunity.

Day 4–5 — Google Ads Restructure

Pause ads for zero-converting products

OP13 (144 sessions, $0), PSA 8 (107 sessions, $0) — wasting ad spend on pages that don't convert. Redirect budget to collection pages.

Send Google Ads to /collections/sealed-boxes

Collection pages convert at 3.64% vs 0.84% homepage. Sealed boxes are your bread and butter — send paid traffic there.

Day 5–7 — Content & Retention Engine

Enable Shopify blog + first SEO post

Organic Search has 73.33% engagement (best channel!) but only 6.5% of traffic. Target: "best japanese pokemon booster boxes australia 2026".

Schedule pack opening Reels for Sundays

You're already filming — post Sundays to drive Monday purchases (Monday = $74.3K, 58% of all orders). Pre-drop hype → Monday buying frenzy.

Build post-purchase email flow (3 emails)

After purchase: thank you + loyalty points reminder → 7 days: related products → 21 days: new drop alert. Turn 203 one-time buyers into repeat customers.

A/B test announcement bar copy

$500 free shipping is working (22.9% of orders qualify, 89% of revenue). Test: "Free shipping over $500" vs "Spend $500+ = Free Shipping + Bonus Card".
