Quick Win Playbook — Do In Order

Each action is backed by verified Shopify Reports + GA4 data. Ordered by revenue impact and effort.

Day 1 — Quick Wins (Already Have the Tools)

Add Instagram link to site footer + header

Footer has zero social links right now. 428 Instagram visitors are already coming — make it easy for website visitors to find your pack opening content.

Promote Appstle loyalty program on product pages

Loyalty widget is installed but not prominent. Only 7.2% repeat rate. Add "Earn X points on this purchase" on product pages.

Optimise cart recovery email sequence

99 abandoned checkouts in 90 days at $272 AOV. 3-email sequence: 1hr (reminder), 6hr (social proof), 24hr (urgency + $10 off).

Day 2-3 — Google Ads Restructure

Pause ads for zero-converting products

OP13 (152 views, $0 revenue), PSA 8 (137 views, $90) — wasting ad spend. Redirect budget to what works.

Send Google Ads to /collections/sealed-boxes

Collection pages convert at 3.64% vs 0.84% homepage. Send paid traffic to collections, not individual products.

Launch Yu-Gi-Oh! Google Shopping campaign

YGO converts at $104/view vs $2.50/view for Pokemon (GA4). Currently only 45 views — doubling could add $4,700+.

Day 4-5 — Retention & Upsells

Create "Bundle Deals" to push orders toward $500

AOV is $272. Create "2-box bundles" or "Complete Set" bundles that land at $500+ to unlock free shipping.

Build post-purchase email flow (3 emails)

After purchase: thank you + loyalty points → 7 days: related products → 21 days: new drop alert.

Day 5-7 — Content & SEO Engine

Enable Shopify blog + first SEO post

Organic Search has 73.33% engagement but only 6.5% of traffic. Target: "best japanese pokemon booster boxes australia 2026".

Schedule pack opening Reels for Sundays

Post Sundays to drive Monday purchases. Pre-drop hype. Link directly to product pages in bio.

Investigate high return rate

$2,540 in returns (17.8% of gross) is eating into margins. Check which products are returned most.

A/B test announcement bar copy

Test: "Free delivery on orders over $500" vs "Spend $500+ = Free Shipping + Bonus Card".
