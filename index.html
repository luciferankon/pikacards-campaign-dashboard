Quick Win Playbook — Do In Order

Each action is backed by verified Shopify Reports + GA4 data. Ordered by revenue impact and effort.

Day 1 — Quick Wins (Already Have the Tools)

Add Instagram link to site footer + header

Footer has zero social links right now. 428 Instagram visitors are already coming — make it easy for website visitors to find your pack opening content.

Promote Appstle loyalty program on product pages

Loyalty widget is installed but not prominent. Only 7.2% repeat rate. Add "Earn X points on this purchase" on product pages to drive signups and return visits.

Optimise cart recovery email sequence

99 abandoned checkouts in 90 days at $272 AOV. Test 3-email sequence: 1hr (reminder), 6hr (social proof from eBay reviews), 24hr (urgency + $10 off).

Day 2–3 — Google Ads Restructure

Pause ads for zero-converting products

OP13 (152 views, $0 revenue), PSA 8 (137 views, $90) — wasting ad spend on pages that don't convert. Redirect budget to what works.

Send Google Ads to /collections/sealed-boxes

Collection pages convert at 3.64% vs 0.84% homepage. Sealed boxes are your bread and butter — send paid traffic to collections, not individual products.

Launch Yu-Gi-Oh! Google Shopping campaign

YGO converts at $104/view vs $2.50/view for Pokemon (GA4 data). Currently getting only 45 views — even doubling that to 90 views could add $4,700+ in revenue.

Day 4–5 — Retention & Upsells

Create "Bundle Deals" to push orders toward $500

AOV is $272 — many orders are in the $200-400 range. Create "2-box bundles" or "Complete Set" bundles that land at $500+ to unlock free shipping.

Build post-purchase email flow (3 emails)

After purchase: thank you + loyalty points reminder → 7 days: related products → 21 days: new drop alert. Turn one-time buyers into repeat customers.

Day 5–7 — Content & SEO Engine

Enable Shopify blog + first SEO post

Organic Search has 73.33% engagement (best channel!) but only 6.5% of traffic. Target: "best japanese pokemon booster boxes australia 2026".

Schedule pack opening Reels for Sundays

You're already filming — post Sundays to drive Monday purchases. Pre-drop hype → Monday buying frenzy. Link directly to product pages in bio.

Investigate high return rate

$2,540 in returns (17.8% of gross) is eating into margins. Check which products are returned most and improve descriptions/photos for those items.

A/B test announcement bar copy

$500 free shipping is the right strategy. Test: "Free delivery on orders over $500" vs "Spend $500+ = Free Shipping + Bonus Card" to see which drives more upsells.
